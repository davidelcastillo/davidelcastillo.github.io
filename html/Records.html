GIBSON RECORDS

Headquartered in Music City, Nashville, TN, Gibson Records partners with artists to produce, record, and promote guitar-driven music to the forefront of culture. Iconic music that has inspired generations of artists has been recorded on Gibson guitars since the inception of recorded music. Today, Gibson Records teams up directly with artists to share their music across the globe.

NEW SLASH SOLO ALBUM-ORGY OF THE DAMNED

Slash’s star-studded debut blues album, Orgy of the Damned, is a collection of 12 dynamic songs that shakes up and revitalizes blues classics with a stripped down, instinctive approach. The acclaimed guitarist and songwriter teamed up with numerous guest vocalists including Iggy Pop, Chris Stapleton, Demi Lovato, Paul Rodgers, Chris Robinson, Billy Gibbons, Gary Clark Jr. Brian Johnson, Beth Hart, Dorothy, and Tash Neal, to create a singular expression that pays homage to the blues.
